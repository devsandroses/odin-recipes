Guacamole

Description

Bold and spicy guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 4 avocados
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tomato, seeded and chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, minced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ½ small red onion, finely chopped
  • Coarse salt and ground pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, lightly mash avocados.
  2. Stir in lime juice, tomato, jalapeño, garlic, red onion, and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
