Tomato Soup

Description

Flavorful and creamy tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, coarsely chopped
  • 2 (14-ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups bone broth
  • Â½ cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium stockpot over medium heat. Cook onion, stirring constantly, until soft and translucent, about 3 minutes.
  2. Add tomatoes, their juices, and stock. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Working in batches, transfer tomato mixture to a blender, food processor, or food mill. Puree tomato mixture.
  4. Return soup to a clean pot and set over low heat.
  5. Whisk in cream, if desired; season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
