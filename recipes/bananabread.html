Banana Bread

Description

Rich and moist banana bread with hints of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter a 9-by-5-by-3-inch loaf pan; set aside. In an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add eggs and beat to incorporate.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda. Add to the butter mixture and mix until just combined.
  4. Add bananas, cinnamon and vanilla; mix to combine.
  5. Pour into prepared pan and bake until a cake tester inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 1 hour 10 minutes.
  6. Let rest in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a rack to cool.
